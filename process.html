How to Commission Me

A quick guide to my workflow so you know exactly what to expect!

1

The Request

Send me a DM with your character references, desired pose, and the type of commission you want (e.g., Full Body). I will review it and confirm the price!

2

Payment & Sketch

Once an invoice is paid via PayPal, I will start the initial sketch. I will send you this sketch for approval, and you can request major changes here.

3

Lineart & Base Colors

After sketch approval, I move on to clean lines and flat colors. I will send one final check-in to make sure the colors are perfect.

4

Final Render

I add shading, lighting, and final details! You will receive a high-resolution PNG file of your completed artwork via email or Google Drive.

Frequently Asked Questions

Usually between 1 to 3 weeks depending on the complexity of the piece and my current queue. I will always keep you updated if there are delays!

Personal commissions are for personal use only! If you need commercial rights for streaming, merch, or branding, please let me know upfront as it requires a commercial fee (usually +50% of the base price).

100% refunds are available ONLY if I have not started the sketch. Once the sketch is approved, I can only offer partial refunds based on how much work has been completed.
